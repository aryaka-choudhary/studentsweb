IIT Mandi welcomes female rank-holders in JEE (Advanced)

Congratulations on your making it into the rank list in the prestigious JEE (Advanced) 2021 exam! This hard-earned feat has opened up for you the possibility of a seat in the BTech or other 4-5 year UG programme in one of the IITs, India's premier and world-renowned engineering institutions.If you are offered a seat in any IIT, it is the best stepping-stone to a rewarding career!

The IITs recognise the sterling role that women engineers can play in the development of Indian society and the importance of a good gender ratio in the classroom.The IIT system has adopted a number of measures to make our UG programmes more attractive to female students.We strongly encourage you to fill in a large number of choices for branches and IITs to maximise your chance of being allotted an IIT seat.

You might have questions about what it is like to be a bright young female student in an IIT.If so, it will be reassuring for you to talk to a senior female student at an IIT.We have teams of women faculty/students/alumni at several IITs who are ready to answer your queries.If you wish to take advantage of this help, please visit one of the links below.There you will find contact information of IIT faculty, students and alumni with whom you can speak for advice on your opportunities at IIT.This help is available by email, telephone and social media.

Taking advice from this helpdesk is entirely voluntary.The advice is only for your information, the decision on which choices to fill is entirely yours.Making it to the rank list does not guarantee you a seat in the IITs. Whether or not you are offered a seat will depend on your list of choices and your rank in the merit list as per the seat allotment business rules.

Once again, my personal congratulations to you.I urge you to fill in a large number of choices in IITs, and hope that you are one of the lucky candidates to be allotted a seat of your choice! With warm regards & best wishes for a rewarding future!

Prof. Ajit Kumar Chaturvedi
Director, IIT Mandi
