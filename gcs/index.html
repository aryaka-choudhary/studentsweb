About Us

Reasons for seeking the help of gcs

We at the Guidance and Counseling Service offer our students the hand of friendship. You do not need a reason to visit us. If you have a concern or a problem be it academic, social, emotional or psychological, you can talk to us in confidence. At GCS, one is valued and accepted in a non-judgmental way. In fact, the aim of GCS is to help the student find his/her own answer.

THIS SERVICE IS FREE OF COST FOR ALL REGISTERED STUDENTS OF IIT MANDI.

    Transitioning from school to college can be very overwhelming for most students. It is the first time for many where they have to leave their parents and live for the next 4 years in a hostel with people they have never met before in their life. Fortunately, in online classes, in spite of its several disadvantages, the transition is smoother. This is where Guidance and Counselling Service plays a big part. When I first got admitted in IIT Mandi, I had several apprehensions as to how the college was in terms of academics, clubs, societies, coding culture and most importantly its remote location. I had been assigned a mentor to guide me through all these. He contacted me quite frequently in the initial weeks of classes and checked if everything was fine. I had a few family problems as well, so I had asked if there were any counsellors whom I could talk with. He provided me with various contacts, which strengthened my belief that mental health was really a major factor and our college was trying its best to improve the mental health of the students. Even now I ask many doubts regarding internships and placements and my mentor answers them to the best of his knowledge. I hope I can be of help to someone as well because I know several students are in my shoes and in dire need of guidance.

    Arkadeep Ghosh, BTech CSE 2020-24, IIT Mandi
